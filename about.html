About Martmax

Rwanda's Premier Electronics Marketplace

Founded in Kigali, Martmax was born from a simple idea: make quality electronics accessible to everyone through a reliable online platform. We connect customers with verified suppliers offering everything from smartphones and gadgets to home appliances and accessories.

Today we've grown into a leading e-commerce destination with over 120 products, competitive pricing in Rwandan Francs, and exceptional customer service. Our commitment remains the same — delivering quality at unbeatable prices with fast, reliable delivery across Kigali and beyond.
